About

Here you can read about why we created Watery, who's behind the project and how you can contact us for future updates

My name is Micha, I'm a second year student at the hogeschool Rotterdam majoring in creative media and game technologies. My goal is to bring creative solutions to everyday problems people face. Watery was created for a project at the hogeschool Rotterdam involving AI and sustainability. We aim to make it easier for people at home (without a garden, living in urban cities) to have (alive!) plants in their home. You probably know the struggle all too well of having your plants keep dying on you. With Watery we hope to make this easier for you. With both our sensors measuring data from your plant and our website making the predictions for you.
