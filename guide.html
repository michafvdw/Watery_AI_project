User guide

below you can read a step by step guide on how to use both the product and our website to

Step 1: make sure the arduino is connected

Make sure the arduino is connected to a power source, whether these are batteries or your own laptop. We already downloaded the code on the arduino.


Step 2: open the console monitor

In the arduino IDE you can look in the console monitor to see the sensor data values coming from your COM poort (make sure you have the right one!)


Step 3: Put the soil moisture sensor in the soil of your plant

The soil moisture sensor (see picture above) is the only sensor you need to move. You need to put this sensor in the soil of your plant to measure the moisture. The temperature sensor and the air humidity sensor can both stay at the same place.


Step 4: Put the data from the monitor in the predict form

When gathering your data its important you do this for at least 5 minutes so you are sure you have stable data points. For each sensor put the amount you saw the most on your monitor in the form. The form can be found here.


Step 5: Predict and take care of your plants!

Once you filled out the form please click on the sumbit button. This will tell you if your plant needs water or not. You can use the website to make predictions for as many plants as you want! If you have any more questions please visit the About for our contact information
